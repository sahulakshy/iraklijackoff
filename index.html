Georgia - More Than Just Peachy!

Welcome to საქართველო (Sakartvelo)!

Not the US state! This Georgia is a country at the intersection of Europe and Asia, home to Caucasus mountain villages and Black Sea beaches.

Fast Facts:

  • Capital: Tbilisi
  • Known for: Cradle of wine (8000+ years!), stunning nature, incredible hospitality, unique alphabet (ანბანი).
  • Must Try: Khachapuri (cheese bread), Khinkali (dumplings), Mtsvadi (BBQ), Churchkhela (nutty candy).

Basically, it's amazing. You should visit! Just be prepared for a *supra* (feast) or two.
